Your Salesforce account will be permanently restricted.

A Trailblazer account is tied to activities across AppExchange, Events, IdeaExchange, Partner Community, Salesforce Help, Salesforce+, Trailblazer Community, and Trailhead. Deleting your Trailblazer account impacts all these experiences and is irreversible once your account is deleted. Here are some of the ways your account would be affected:



Trailhead
Deletion removes your rank, badges, points, and superbadges, and they can't be retrieved. Certifications will remain but will not be associated with your Trailblazer Account.
Trailblazer Community
Posts you wrote remain visible after you delete your account, but they show as posted by an anonymous alias. If you want your posts removed, delete them before deleting your account. Connections you made and groups you follow will be deleted and can't be retrieved.
Salesforce Help
Deleting your Trailblazer account doesn't automatically delete associated support case data.
